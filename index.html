About Me

Hi and welcome to my Portfolio website.
My name is Eduardo and I am a 42-year-old Portuguese man born and raised in Lisbon.
My professional dream is to become a successful, highly skilled web-developer.
Two years ago I found myself experimenting with creating a website and a passion for learning code emerged.
Before this, I have had a highly successful career in the hospitality sector, working my way up to Head Sommelier and Restaurant Director in high-end luxury venues. A career that was full of memorable moments and achievements like being awarded Sommelier of the year by Wine Magazine or placing in the top 3 for 3 consecutive years in the most prestigious national sommelier competition in my country. My long and successful career in hospitality will hopefully be very useful in terms of transferable soft skills to my new and ambitious professional goals.



Since I discover how happy coding made me feel, I've been learning as much as I can from online courses, tutorials and numerous websites from Stack Overflow to W3Schools or MDN.


Finally, the opportunity arose with the first coding Bootcamp in the UK promoted by Trilogy at the University of Birmingham. Excited with the perspective of having structured and intense learning, I immediately enrolled and I am currently in the initial stage of the course.


In this Portfolio website, I will be posting the projects I will be making in Bootcamp. For now, and just for fun, I have posted some websites I have made before starting the course and that are currently live. These ones were made using website builders, so they don't realy represent my ambition, but I hope you like them anyway.
